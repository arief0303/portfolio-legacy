The Interview

Sprint 0

Interview Preparation

The purpose of the interview is to find out the needs of the client's target audience. This can be about the artist themself, the genre of the music the target audience is interested in, or even matters outside music that contributes to the experience such as what are the platform are the audience uses to listen and many other.

Preparing the form

As part of the exercise, a questionnaire is prepared in order to gather data from the target audience. This questionnaire covers basic questions to get the general idea of how to target audience is.

Interview Result

Conclusions from the interview reveals:

•Our client’s target group is predominantly constituted out of women.

•Age ranges between 14–19 years old

•We can find this group being active in social media such as Instagram, Snapchat, etc.
